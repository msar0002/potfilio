About Me

Greetings! My name is Osama Msarwah, and I hail from the beautiful country of Jordan. I am a 25-year-old web developer with a passion for creating immersive online experiences. I pursued my Bachelor's degree in Jordan at the esteemed University of Science and Technology in Irbid. After successfully completing my studies, I embarked on a rewarding journey as a web developer at Double Support, a prominent company in Amman, Jordan. During my tenure there, I focused on back-end development for the mobile team, honing my skills and gaining valuable industry experience. Seeking further growth and opportunities to challenge myself, I made the exciting decision to venture to Canada. My objective was to enhance my expertise and acquire a deeper understanding of interactive media design. Thus, I enrolled in the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College. Through this program, I aim to develop my abilities in website design and construct web applications from the ground up. The program is slated to conclude in 2024, and I am eagerly looking forward to applying my newfound knowledge and skills. As I approach graduation, I am enthusiastic about the prospect of finding a rewarding cooperation job where I can contribute my expertise and collaborate with fellow professionals to create remarkable digital experiences. My journey has been filled with determination and a passion for innovation, and I am excited about the future possibilities that lie ahead. With a dedication to continuous improvement and a commitment to excellence, I am eager to make a positive impact in the world of web development and design. Thank you for visiting my page, and I hope to connect with you soon!
